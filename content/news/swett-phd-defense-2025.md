title: "Nicky Swett successfully defends PhD"
date: 2025-01-14
summary: "Nicky Swett has successfully defended his PhD thesis on Hans Keller's Wordless Functional Analysis as a Theory of Musical Comparison."
---

We are very pleased to share that Nicky Swett, CMS PhD student, has successfully defended his PhD thesis titled 'Functional Analogies: Hans Keller's Wordless Functional Analysis as a Theory of Musical Comparison'. This work investigates Hans Keller's innovative 15 Functional Analyses from the perspective of cognitive psychology, seeking to understand the psychological mechanisms underlying musical comparison.
