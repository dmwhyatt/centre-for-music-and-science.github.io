Our new paper entitled "Jazz Trio Database: Automated Annotation of Jazz Piano Trio Recordings Processed Using Audio Source Separation" is just published in Transactions of the International Society of Music Information Retrieval (TISMIR). This paper arises from work completed by Huw Cheston during his PhD at the CMS, in collaboration with Joshua Schlichting at the Hochschule für Musik und Theater Hamburg.
