The 2026 SEMPRE PGR/ECR Conference will be held on Thursday 16th April 2026 for postgraduate and early career researchers (PGRs/ECRs) in the fields of music psychology and music education. It will be jointly hosted by the Centre for Music and Science (University of Cambridge) and the Music, Mind and Brain group (Goldsmiths College, University of London).
